Put your .mp3 audio files in this folder.

A file named  my-song.mp3  placed here becomes reachable at:
  /audio/my-song.mp3

Use that path as the "url" in prisma/seed.mjs.

Only use music you have the right to use (royalty-free / Creative Commons),
since this app is deployed publicly. Good sources:
  - Pixabay Music        https://pixabay.com/music/
  - Free Music Archive   https://freemusicarchive.org/
  - Incompetech          https://incompetech.com/
